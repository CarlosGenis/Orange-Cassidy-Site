Who is Orange Cassidy?

James Cipperly (born May 4, 1984), better known by his ring name Orange Cassidy, is an American professional wrestler, currently signed to All Elite Wrestling (AEW).The best way to describe Orange Cassidy is as a "lazy pro wrestler" which has earned him the nickname of "King of Sloth Style"

Previous Ring Names: Fire Ant and JC Ryder
